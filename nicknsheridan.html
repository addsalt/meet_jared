Nick Jays and Sheridan Grout

Nick Jays and Sheridan Grout are local House and Techno DJs. They approached me with the idea to join forces and create a team identity with which they could promote themselves. The slick sound and deep grooves reminded me of the Miami club culture which steered my direction into a edgy Miami Vice look and feel. The geometric typography was hand rendered using a grid and precise angles, and a hell of a lot of Gestalt.
