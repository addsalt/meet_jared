Frontcrush

Frontcrush is a start-up development company once again located in Peru. The owner needed a clean, approachable and corporate identity for his brand, as well as the face for the website. Our solution was to keep it simple, techy and abstract as to be as versatile as possible.

The logo has an icon and wordmark which can be used separately. Simplicity and professionalism were the two main factors of focus. We also created custom icons and graphics for his website to really create a personality for it. I think we achieved just that.

This was another collaboration with my partner Daniela Chavez and is still in development. Link to come soon!
