Multiplica Tus Clientes

Multiplica Tus Clientes was created by a web development agency in Peru whom my partner and I often work with. The idea is to drive sales and help them better their client's user experience by optimizing SEO using Google Adwords and other tools. They wanted a simple site with character, to inform clients and users about everything available to them to make the best of their website.

We created a friendly little retro character that welcomes and walks users through the explanation of Multiplica's services. He's friendly and inviting in order to ensure users that they have the solution
