Casarte and Villart

This was an interesting project. There were two facets to it. The first being an online art gallery, and the second being the same concept, but specifically for couples getting married. Villart is an online art gallery where people can consult about pieces they find and purchase them. Casarte, on the other hand, works more like a registry, where friends and family can contribute to selected pieces in a couple's wishlist, and collectively purchase the piece. We had the opportunity to work on both the brand and website.

Here you can see the Casarte and Villart logos which are similar in look in order to maintain a synchronized feel throughout each platform. It was important to have a clean and elegant look with a touch of the painter's hand. We've acomplished this by using customized, elegant typography with a painter's stroke to cross the T. This was also a collaboration with my partner Daniela Chavez and the site is currently still in development stages. Link to come soon!
