Westboro Workers Co-operative

This cooperative was an initiative in the Westboro area to get businesses involved in donating to CHEO while encouraging local commerce. The logo needed to convey a community spirit and represent many different types of people and businesses. By combining cute hand-drawn icons and typography, we conveyed the message: "We are a cooperative!"

Although the initiative was never put into fruition, you can visit the sign up form which my partner in crime and I designed. This was a collaboration project with my partner Daniela Chavez. Check it out!
