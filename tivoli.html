Tivoli

Tivoli Florist is a local florist shop in Ottawa. They pride themselves in having some of the most unique arrangments, and ship in flowers from all over the world for their pieces. I admired their work ethic and dedication to beauty, so I selected them as the company I would rebrand for a class project. I ended up with two results that I really loved so the choice was difficult, but I inevitably narrowed it down to this one.
